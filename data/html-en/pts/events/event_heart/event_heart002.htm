Event Manager Buzz the Cat:
First, you must gather heart pieces.
You can get them off of monsters all throughout the world. You will find that the pieces are numbered 1-9. Obtain one of each of the nine differently numbered pieces. You are then qualified to participate.
It's a simple game, where you choose rock, paper, or scissors. After each round you decide whether to continue to the next stage or accept your current prizes and quit. When you lose, the game ends. Your prize is determined by how many times in a row you won. Losing will reduce the quality of your prize, so don't be afraid to quit while you are ahead.
