You cannot damage the spirit of fire with regular attacks.
So in all her leniency, Eva decided that we were worthy enough to receive some of her power.
When the spirit of fire is summoned, all adventurers near the spirit will receive some of Eva’s power.
With this power, adventurers will be able to attack the spirit of fire.
Keep hitting until the spirit grows weak and eventually dies. Once the spirit dies, you can receive a reward.
