Alchemist's Servitor:
The Red Philosopher's Stone requires: 1 Transmutation Circle, 20 Magic Reagents, 10 Philosopher's Stone Ore and 1 Conversion Formula.
The Blue Philosopher's Stone requires: 1 Transmutation Circle, 30 Magic Reagents, 5 Philosopher's Stone Ore and 1 Conversion Formula.
The Orange Philosopher's Stone requires: 1 Transmutation Circle, 24 Magic Reagents, 8 Philosopher's Stone Ore and 1 Conversion Formula.
The Black Philosopher's Stone requires: 1 Transmutation Circle, 28 Magic Reagents, 6 Philosopher's Stone Ore and 1 Conversion Formula.
The White Philosopher's Stone requires: 1 Transmutation Circle, 12 Magic Reagents, 14 Philosopher's Stone Ore and 1 Conversion Formula.
The Green Philosopher's Stone requires: 1 Transmutation Circle, 6 Magic Reagents, 17 Philosopher's Stone Ore and 1 Conversion Formula.
With these colored Philosopher’s Stones, it is possible to make A-Grade weapons, hair accessories that grant temporary magical effects, Blessed Scrolls of Resurrection, Blessed Scrolls of Escape, buff scrolls, Quick Healing Potions and Greater Healing Potions.
