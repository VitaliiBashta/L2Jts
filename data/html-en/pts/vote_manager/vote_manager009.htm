Priest of Blessing:
Nevit's Voice is an item that increases the amount of Recommendations you can give to others by 10.
The 20 hour donation timer starts from the time of your last donation.
In addition, this waiting time is shared by all characters on an account. Meaning that if you donate with one character that all characters will have to wait the 20 hours.
However, there is no usage waiting time. Meaning you can use as many as you wish at the same time.
