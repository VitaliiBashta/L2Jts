Guard Kent:
Reeds have taken over the marshland in recent years. So much so that it is no longer called Innadril Marshland. Now it is known as the Field of Reeds. This road will take you to the Field of Whispers. Further north you will find the Field of Silence.
They say the reeds there hold their breaths at the arrival of the new goddess.
