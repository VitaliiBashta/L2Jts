TIP
You can change the quality of your Screenshot in the Options Menu. Select Options -> Screen Information -> and select either Bitmap, High, Average, or Low from the Screenshot dropdown list.
How to take a Screenshot
You can take a Screenshot by pressing the 'Print Screen' key on your keyboard.
Screenshot Location
Screenshots are automatically saved to your Lineage II game directory in the Screenshots folder.
Toggle HUD
In order to toggle on/off the UI so you have those windows ruining your shot, press ALT + H.
