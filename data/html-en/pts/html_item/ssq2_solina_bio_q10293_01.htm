Solina's Biography - Aden Royal Philologist:
Solina was an Elmore - Aden imperial princess and older sister of Frintezza, who was the last emperor. She was also the first daughter of Baium to display power so great it rivaled that of the Great Emperor Shunaiman.
She is remembered because she was a member of the Elmore - Aden imperial family, she had extraordinary attributes and characteristics, and her life saved the human race from the wrath of the gods.
