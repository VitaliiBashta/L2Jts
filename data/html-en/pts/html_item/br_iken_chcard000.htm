Name: Iken
"This is not time to sit still! Onward!"
Level/Occupation: 25 Fighter
Personality/Characteristics: Outgoing personality. Slightly careless. Thick-headed. Slow when gathering information. Still hasn't done a class transfer. When still, his lip slightly sticks out like he is pouting.
Dream: Wants to become a dignified Phoenix Knight. Is into heroic stories. Looks up to good-looking people.
Likes: Likes to run. Likes to play with a ball. Likes cool breezes.


Side Chapter #1

"What?! Really?" Iken shouted out, right in the middle of the Talking Island village square. But this was no time for silence - he could really become a knight! A knight in shining armor with a sword in one hand and a shield in the other... A brave knight who protects the people behind him.... He puffed up with pride... it had always been Iken's dream to become a knight. The only problem, of course, was that no one ever had ever told him how exactly to go about becoming a knight....
He would travel out regardless, fighting monsters and saving villagers and all that, when one day he met a beautiful Elf around the ancient Elven Relics. They were out adventuring together when the Elf casually told him that he could become a knight whenever he wanted.

"Iken, you're already qualified enough to become a knight! You should pass the apprentice knight test without any problems." Iken was amazed.
"There... there's a test for becoming a knight?" The Elf giggled, nodding slightly.
"Hoo! You really don't know anything about it? You have to take the apprentice knight test in Gludin Village." Iken flashed a smile, and darted back home as fast as he could.
.
.
That evening, Iken gazed up at the stars, his excited eyes joining dots together and making patterns in the night sky. He didn't get one wink of sleep.
Oh... to become a knight! During lunch the following day, he promised the kind and gentle Elf that they would take a boat together to Gludin Village. Riding a boat, becoming a knight! Oh the adventure! Iken was also worried a little bit about seasickness, he had never been on a boat after all. There was also that mysterious male Elf whom he had met yesterday. Why did he disappear from the Elven Relics without saying anything? Iken frowned for a moment... what had happened to him? What if he was hurt? Maybe they should look for... "I'M GOING TO BE A KNIGHT! Woohoo!"
