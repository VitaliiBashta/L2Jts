Saga Chapter #1 White Feathers and the Red Door.

Ash lay scattered where she fell. The sky burned scarlet red, pulsing against the broken trees and shattered stone that lay all around.
This place was different, different but similar. She traced shadows of the Mimir Woods with her eyes; crests and particular trees that she remembered, but this... this wasn't the same.

Snow. It was snowing here. And the people... she had never laid eyes on them, their clothes too... they were like none she had ever seen before.

"Where am I?" She felt the cold seeping in through her tattered armor. She felt the apprehensiion seep in too, followed by uncertainty, confusion, fear.
.
.
Moments ago she had been attacked by terrible creatures while strolling through the Mimir Woods. She ran, weaving through trees and shrubs as beasts butchered her comrades. They had gotten to her too: she was hurt and slowing fast... her injuries fast taking hold over her senses and will to escape, to survive. She staggered forward, just reaching the edge of the woods when suddenly a blinding red light engulfed her. She opened her eyes and found a strange, alien place before her.

Naonin was overwhelmed by all the people surrounding her, speaking in tongues that she could not understand. Her will to run, to escape was still strong, but while the mind was willing, the body was too hurt. She collapsed.
