Newbie Bonus
Once you reach level 6, your character becomes a Newbie and is eligible to receive a number of benefits until you reach level 24.
Benefits include:
Equipment Exchange -
At weapons, armor or accessories shops, you will receive the margin when exchanging equipment. All non-grade equipment can be exchanged.
Support Magic -
Guides in villages will cast support magic according to your level (ranges from level 8 to level 39).
Additional Quest Bonuses -
You may receive additional bonus in certain quests.
