Newbie Guide:
At this level, if you have a preferred attack skill, train hard to develop it. Upgrade the ones you already use most when you reach Level 10.
You usually learn support skills after acquiring attack skills, so now is the time to learn skills such as Armor Mastery, Weapon Mastery, Magic Immunity and Soul Mastery. If you already learned those, it would be advisable to acquire Rapid Attack, which temporarily increases your attack speed.
