Newbie Guide:
You can hunt almost everywhere on the Island at your level. Your main targets should probably be a Grotto Golem or a Shady Muertos. You can find monsters in Nornil's Cave, in the northeastern part of the town.
If you crave a more difficult challenge, go to the southwest of the cave where you will find the Nornil's Garden. It is the center of the Island...and an extremely dangerous place. If you are in a party, you may attempt to penetrate it.
