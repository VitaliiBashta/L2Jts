Newbie Guide:
To congratulate you on your achievements, the Adventure Guild is pleased to open its warehouse to both apprentice adventurers who have reached level 6 as well as to travelers who have completed their first class transfer. At level 6, you can use the Apprentice Adventurer's Weapon Exchange Coupon to borrow a No-Grade Shadow Weapon. At level 20, you can use the Adventurer's Magic Accessory Exchange Coupon to borrow Shadow Armor. No-Grade Shadow Weapons can be exchanged up to level 19, and Shadow Armor can be exchanged up to level 39. So how can I help you?
(Chaotic characters may not use weapons or armor borrowed from the Adventure Guild.)
Receive the Apprentice Adventurer's Weapon Exchange Coupon.
Select a No-Grade Shadow Weapon.
Receive the Adventurer's Magic Accessory Exchange Coupon.
Select Magic Accessory.
